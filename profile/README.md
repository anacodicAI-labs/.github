# AnacodicAI Labs

We build production AI systems and faculty-backed research for
communities that need access to technology and create the
environment for students to do that work for real.

Rooted at **Boston University**. 

---

## What we build

**Research**
Faculty-advised research threads in AI, ML, and systems. Published at MDPI · ACM · IEEE. 

**Projects**
Production systems deployed for real users:  education, healthcare, and livelihoods. 

**Community**
Students, researchers, and engineers contributing across all tracks. Every contribution documented.

---

## Get involved

Three ways to contribute:

📖 **Learn:** Work through the curriculum and publish tutorials or guides with your byline. 

[Open Learning Hub →](https://anacodicai.com/learn)

🔬 **Research:** Join a faculty-backed research thread. Run experiments, write sections, earn co-authorship.

[See open research →](https://anacodicai.com/research)

🛠 **Projects:** Join a production project with real users and constraints.

[View open projects →](https://anacodicai.com/projects)
